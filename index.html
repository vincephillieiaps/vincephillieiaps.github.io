My Booklist

  • Looking for some new books for your child to read or for you to read to your child? This is a list of quality books that are available at either the Pittsford Public Library and/or Jefferson Road Library. I am including an approximate reading level. Since your child is most likely not reading at grade level, take this into consideration by selecting books at their reading level and not grade level. For example, your child may be in 2nd grade, but reading at a mid-1st grade level. If you have any questions about what level your child is reading, please email me.

    Since not all books are leveled equally, please use the five finger rule. If your child gets stuck on 5 or more words on the first page, then this is too hard. Also use your best judgment. If your child is getting frustrated, then it is too hard. Reading easier books is always encouraged as this builds fluency and self-confidence.

    This page is a work in progress, so please continue to check back as more books will be added.

ALSC Notable Children's Books

  • A Boy and a Jaguar

    by Alan Rabinowitz Year Published: Challenging

    Alan loves animals, but the great cat house at the Bronx Zoo makes him sad. Why are they all alone in empty cages? Are they being punished? More than anything, he wants to be their champion—their voice—but he stutters uncontrollably. Except when he talks to animals… Then he is fluent. Follow the life of the man Time Magazine calls, "the Indiana Jones of wildlife conservation "as he searches for his voice and fulfills a promise to speak for animals, and people, who cannot speak for themselves. This real-life story with tender illustrations by Catia Chen explores truths not defined by the spoken word. Reading Level-4th grade (Good Read aloud) Available-Pittsford Public Library and JRE Library.

     

    Note: This book is available in our Library.
